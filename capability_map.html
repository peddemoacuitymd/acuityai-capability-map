Known Limitations
Large-Scale Analysis
AcuityAI works best for targeted queries — individual HCPs, specific accounts, and focused lists. It can't analyze hundreds of HCPs at once or process territory-wide bulk requests comprehensively.
Predictive Trends
AcuityAI surfaces historical data and current snapshots, but can't predict how an HCP's clinical volume or practice patterns will trend over time. Forecasting and projections are not yet supported.
Persona Gaps
AcuityAI is built primarily for field sales reps today. Distributors have no dedicated support. National account managers face a significant gap — there are no IDN-level tools for managing health system relationships, contracts, or cross-facility strategy.
